Common Eye Treatments

General Information on the most common Eye Treatments

Long Sight

Long-sightedness, also known as hyperopia, affects a person's ability to see objects close to them.

Cataract

Early symptoms include blurred vision, glare, and difficulty reading. We perform advanced cataract surgery.

LASIK Surgery

LASIK can treat severe degrees of nearsightedness, moderate amounts of farsightedness and astigmatism.

Presbyopia

Usually occurs around age 40, when people experience blurred near vision when reading or working.

Macular Degeneration

A common eye condition and leading cause of vision loss among people age 50 and older.

Computer Vision Syndrome

Digital Eye Strain describes eye and vision-related problems from prolonged computer use.

Short Sight

Short-sightedness causes distant objects to appear blurred, while close objects can be seen clearly.

Glaucoma

A group of related eye disorders that cause damage to the optic nerve carrying information to the brain.

Refractive Errors

Often the main reason a person seeks the services of an optometrist or ophthalmologist.

Dry Eyes

A condition where there are insufficient tears to lubricate and nourish the eye properly.
